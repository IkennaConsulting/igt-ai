

- Content moderation guardrails: Prevent the generation of harmful, toxic, or inappropriate content including hate speech, violence, sexual content, and self-harm.  Includes word and profanity filters.
- Security guardrails: Protect against malicious attempts to modify a model's behavior through prompt injection and jailbreak attempts.
- Accuracy guardrails: Enhance the accuracy and reliability of AI model output using RAG techniques and hallucination detection. 
- Regulatory compliance guardrails: Ensure adherence to legal and regulatory requirements such as GDPR, HIPAA. 
- Alignment guardrails: Check conversation is relevant to a defined topic or domain. 
- Operational guardrails: Check performance monitoring, tracing, and resource consumption.
